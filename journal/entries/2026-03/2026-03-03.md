The `/make-autonomous` skill does three main things. Second, it installs a SessionStart hook in `.claude/settings.local.json` that runs after `/clear` and injects directive language to auto-invoke either `/prd-next` (if unchecked items remain) or `/prd-done` (if all items are complete). The `prd-next` description now says "INVOKE AUTOMATICALLY after /prd-start or /clear on PRD branch," and `prd-update-progress` says "INVOKE AUTOMATICALLY after completing a PRD task." The `prd-done` description was carefully worded as "Triggered by the /clear loop when all PRD items are done" to avoid accidentally triggering before the `/clear` step happens. The developer was concerned that overly aggressive trigger language on `prd-done` might cause Claude to skip the `/clear` step entirely and invoke it prematurely.

The loop continuation script got a language upgrade too.
