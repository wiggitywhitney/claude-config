- Standalone scripts (safety hooks) don't need separate symlinking — they're already in `scripts/` and referenced via resolved `$CLAUDE_CONFIG_DIR` paths in the settings template.
- Symlink idempotency: correct links skipped, wrong links updated, regular files/dirs cause hard errors to prevent data loss.
